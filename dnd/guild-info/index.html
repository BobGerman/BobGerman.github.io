    Guild of the Golden Dragon

    Crossroads Inn

    At the crossing of the Old North Road and Kings March sits the aptly named Crossroads Inn, a welcome sight to weary travelers. The innkeeper, Orrin Moonshard, is an elderly wood elf with white hair.

    On one side, through an inconspicuous entrance, is the Guild Hall, the meeting place of the Guild of the Golden Dragon. The Guild is a loose alliance of adventurers who work together to keep the roads safe from monsters, bandits, and other threats. They also sometimes help neighboring settlers and carry out special missions, which often bring rich rewards. Orrin is the defacto organizer of the Guild, however he never leaves the inn as he is its sole proprietor. He often catches wind of adventures and informs the Guild members. Orrin maintains a flock of messenger pigeons in a coop behind the inn which he uses to communicate with the members and call them together.

    The campaign is an anthology of one-shot to 2 or 3-shot adventures. This allows for reduced commitment to make every session, and for more variety at the expense of a big story arc.

    Episodes

    1. Light the Way - The party assists in illuminating a dark path through Duskmire forest and encounters bandits and a number of pests

    2. Whispers Beneath the Mire - The party assists a swamp hunter who has encountered unnatural creatures. They end up finding a chapel in the town of Marrowfen, now buried in mud, where someone has set up a chapel to the god of death and decay, Moander.

    3. Wave of Destruction - The party assists a druid, Elaris Aldersill, in opening supply lines from the enchanted Eirwin Island. The party discovers a 2-headed Plesiosaurus is guarding the island, and preventing boats from approaching the island. They befriend the beast and the elder and cleric who live there.

    4. Special Delivery - The Guild is asked to deliver a package to Nexus Lab, an arctic research station. They arrive to discover the scientsts have infected themselves with the serum they have developed, and it turned them into ogres. The party manages to make and deliver the antidote, and they resolve to confront Lord vey who is funding the research and may be developing an army of monsters.

    5. Blackstone Manor - The party proceed to Lord Vey's residence where they are met by his sargeant of arms, a dwarven fighter. She asks them to wait in the sitting room and - it attacks them with animated swords and armor and a smothering rug. They explore the Manor and find a portal to Nexus Prime on the plane of Mechanus, where Lord vey is indeed building an army of monsters - robotic ones (constructs).

    6. Nexus Prime - The party returns to Mechanus with two officers from the Blackstone guard. They manage to disable most of Vey's lab and factory, and they find Lord Vey and Elmira. The officers arrest them and return with them to Blackstone Harbor.

    7. Foes in the Foliage - The treant Puna has been poisoned by hooded creatures and Elaris seeks the party's assistance. To make the healing tonic, the party had to obtain a rare flower from the top of the Mother Tree, where they encounter giant wasps, a constrictor snake, and a number of boggles, all while being fired on by dark cultists. They then fight a rot ogre, zombies, and more cultists to rescue the Treant. The evil druid escapes by turning to Gaseous Form.

    8. Garden of Decay Part 1 - The party makes a new friend, Twigs the Fiary Bard. She tells them she tracked the druid, and that he has a hydra in the swamp that is poisoning the water. The party is joined by Elaris and Puna the Treant to confront the hydra. They manage to defeat it, along with Beozuk, a shadow demon who is waiting there, and a number of dretches. When it finally seems safe, they begin to explore and are confronted with a huge sentinel tree outside of the villain's cave.
