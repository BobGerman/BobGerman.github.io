# Pan-fried Salmon

Perfect salmon with cripsed skin using a Nuwave induction burner

## Equipment

 - Nuwave induction burner with probe
 - Induction-ready frying pan with cover
 - Fish spatula

## Ingredients

 - Salmon filet with skin on
   ideally room temperature
 - Peanut or avocado oil
 - Balsamic reduction
 - Sesame seeds
 
## Instructions

1. Set pan to 275°F, add oil
2. Add salmon skin down and insert probe. Cover loosely allowing for probe wire.
3. Set probe to cook to 120°F and wait until done
4. Remove probe and use the spatula to separate the skin from fish and transfer fish to plate(s).
5. Set pan to maximum/sear (575°F) and crisp the skin on both sides for 1-2 minutes
6. Place the skin over the salmon; add a spray of balsamic reduction and a sprinkle of sesame seeds
