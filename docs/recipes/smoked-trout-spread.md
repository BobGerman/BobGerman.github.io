# Smoked Trout Spread

## Equipment

 - Food processor

## Ingredients

 - 2 fillets smoked trout (1/2 lb), skin removed
 - 4 oz cream cheese (half a brick)
 - 2 Tbsp mayonnaise
 - Juice and zest of 1/4 lemon
 - 1 Tbsp prepared horseradish
 - 1 bunch scallions (5 or 6)
 - 2 Tbsp capers in brine, drained
 - 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
 
## Instructions

1. Clean scallions and remove roots and chop into pieces a few inches long. Place in food processor and chop finely.

2. Add the other ingredients except for the trout and blend thoroughly

3. Cut trout into 2-3" pieces and add to food processor. Pulse several times until trout is blended but there are still some chunky pieces.

4. Transfer to a bowl and enjoy

Store for up to a week in the refrigerator
