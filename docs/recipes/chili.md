# Bob's Chili

Classic chili with dried or canned beans, adapted from https://savorthebest.com/chili-beans-with-beef/

## Equipment

 - Dutch oven or stock pot

## Ingredients

 - 1 lb dried beans OR 4 x 14.5oz cans of plain beans
 - 2 lb ground beef (or turkey or pork)
 - 1 onion chopped
 - 4 cloves garlic chopped
 - 2 jalapeno peppers de-seeded and chopped
 - 1/2 chopped cilantro (or 1 Tbsp dried)
 - 3 x 14o z cans crushed tomatoes
 - 1 x 6 oz can tomato paste
 - 1 chipolte pepper in adobo sauce mashed to a paste (not the whole can! Freeze for future use)
 - 2 cups beef stock
 - 2 Tbsp chili powder
 - 1 Tbsp dried oregano
 - 2 tsp ground cumin
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 1 tsp ground black pepper
 - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  
## Instructions

1. Cook dried beans as directed, drane and rinse, OR Rinse canned beans in a colender

2. In a large Dutch oven brown the ground meat and break it up with a wooden spoon.

3. Add chopped onion, garlic, and jalapenos and sautee until onions are transluscent

4. Add the remaining ingredients except the beans and stir to combine thoroughly

5. Add the beans, stir, and bring to a boil

6. Cover and reduce heat; simmer for 1 1/2 - 2 hours
