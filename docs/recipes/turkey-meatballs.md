# Turkey Meatballs

## Ingredients

| ~ 1 oz Meatballs | 24 | 48 | 72 | 
|--|--|--|--|
| Ground turkey, 93% lean | 1 lb | 2 lb | 3 lb |
| Plain bread crumbs | 1/2 cup | 1 cup | 1 1/2 cups |
| Onion, grated or finely minced | 1/3 cup | 2/3 cup | 1 cup |
| Garlic | 2 cloves | 4 cloves | 6 cloves |
| Eggs, large | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| Olive oil | 1 Tbsp | 2 Tbsp | 3 Tbsp |
| Worcestershire sauce | 1 tsp | 2 tsp | 3 tsp |
| Dried sage | 1/4 tsp | 1/2 tsp | 3/4 tsp |
| Dried marjoram | 1/4 tsp | 1/2 tsp | 3/4 tsp |
| Ground black pepper | 1/4 tsp | 1/2 tsp | 3/4 tsp |
| Oil for frying | 1/4 cup | 3/8 cup | 1/2 cup |

## Instructions

1. Mix wet ingredients in a small bowl, then mix with all ingredients in a large bowl with a large spoon or clean hands
2. Shape into 1" balls
3. Heat oil over medium-high (350°F) heat in a large skillet. Fry meatballs in batches on all sides, 5-7 min (165°F inside)

They freeze well on a cookie sheet to keep them separated.
